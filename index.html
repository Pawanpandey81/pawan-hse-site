Professional profile

I work as an HSE Manager in the power generation sector. My daily work includes audits, risk assessments, safety plans, incident investigations, and coaching supervisors on safe work.

I specialise in mill maintenance and high-risk activities inside power stations. I understand permit-to-work systems, isolation standards, confined spaces, and contractor management.

I write and review SHE plans, baseline risk assessments, and operating procedures for sites such as Medupi, Kusile, Majuba, Duvha, and other Eskom stations.

HSE services

Support for organisations that need both strong documentation and practical field control.

01

Baseline and task-based risk assessments

Development and review of baseline and issue-based risk assessments for power station work, mill maintenance, construction, and workshop activities.

Outcome. Clear risk ratings, control measures, and action plans for supervisors.

02

SHE plans and safety files

Full SHE plans and safety files for tenders, shutdowns, and long-term maintenance contracts, tailored to client specifications and legal duties.

Outcome. Document packs ready for client review and audits.

03

Gap analysis and audits

Site inspections, document reviews, and gap analysis against OHS Act requirements, ISO 45001 structure, and client standards.

Incident investigation support

Assistance with incident investigations, root cause analysis, and action plans. Focus on learning and prevention, not blame.
